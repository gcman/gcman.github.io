Introduction

Welcome to the homepage of my Project Euler solutions. Once I solve the original problem, I also take on the modified version from ProjectEuler+ on Hackerrank, where the problems usually have multiple, harder test cases. The time limits are much stricter, and brute force is rarely an option. I like these problems because to solve them, you have to be clever with not only a good mathematical understanding but also an efficient use of algorithms.
