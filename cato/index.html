Down With Brassicato!

Gautam Manohar

latin 

31 July 2018

#poetry

â€‹

An elegiac couplet I composed to slander Cato the Elder.

res capite infamata peribit Brassicatone.
disce quod ignoras: despicimus Cato, te.

The republic will die disgraced with Cato as Cabbage-Head.
Cato, learn what you do not know: we hate you.
